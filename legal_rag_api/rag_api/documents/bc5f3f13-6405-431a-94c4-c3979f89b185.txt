
        RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

        THIS AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Lease") is made and entered into this 15th day of January, 2021, by and between PROPERTY MANAGEMENT LLC ("Landlord") and JANE DOE ("Tenant").

        1. PREMISES: Landlord hereby leases to Tenant, and Tenant hereby leases from Landlord, the residential premises located at: 123 Main Street, Apt. 4B, San Francisco, CA 94110 (the "Premises").

        2. TERM: The term of this Lease shall be for a period of one (1) year, commencing on February 1, 2021 and ending on January 31, 2022 (the "Initial Term"). This Lease shall automatically renew on a month-to-month basis after the Initial Term unless either party provides written notice of termination at least thirty (30) days prior to the end of the Initial Term or any renewal period.

        3. RENT: Tenant agrees to pay Landlord the sum of $2,800.00 per month as rent for the Premises. Rent shall be due on the 1st day of each month, payable to Property Management LLC at 500 Market Street, Suite 200, San Francisco, CA 94105, or at such other place as Landlord may designate from time to time.

        4. LATE CHARGES: If Tenant fails to pay the rent in full by the 5th day of the month, Tenant shall pay a late charge of $100.00 plus $10.00 for each additional day that the rent remains unpaid.

        5. SECURITY DEPOSIT: Upon execution of this Lease, Tenant shall deposit with Landlord the sum of $3,000.00 as a security deposit, which shall be held and applied in accordance with California Civil Code Section 1950.5.

        6. RENT INCREASES: Any increase in the monthly rent during the term of the Lease or any renewal period shall require at least thirty (30) days' written notice to Tenant. Any rent increase must comply with the San Francisco Rent Ordinance and other applicable laws.

        7. UTILITIES: Tenant shall be responsible for paying all utilities and services for the Premises, including electricity, gas, water, sewer, trash collection, telephone, and internet.

        8. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS: Tenant shall maintain the Premises in a clean and sanitary condition and shall properly use and operate all appliances, fixtures, and equipment. Tenant shall immediately notify Landlord of any defects or dangerous conditions in and about the Premises. Landlord shall make all necessary repairs with reasonable promptness at Landlord's expense, except for repairs required due to Tenant's misuse or neglect.

        9. ALTERATIONS: Tenant shall not make any alterations, additions, or improvements to the Premises without the prior written consent of Landlord.

        10. ENTRY AND INSPECTION: Landlord shall have the right to enter the Premises in accordance with California Civil Code Section 1954.

        11. GOVERNING LAW: This Lease shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California and the San Francisco Rent Ordinance.

        IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Lease as of the date first above written.

        LANDLORD: Property Management LLC

        Title: Managing Member
